PERSONAL INSURANCE PRODUCTS

AUTO INSURANCE

Our auto insurance provides comprehensive protection for your vehicle and financial security when you're on the road.

COVERAGE OPTIONS:
- Liability Coverage: Pays for injuries or property damage you cause to others
- Collision Coverage: Pays to repair or replace your vehicle after an accident
- Comprehensive Coverage: Covers non-collision damage (theft, vandalism, weather, fire, etc.)
- Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist: Protects you when hit by a driver with insufficient coverage
- Medical Payments: Covers medical expenses for you and your passengers
- Personal Injury Protection (PIP): Covers medical expenses and lost wages (required in some states)

OPTIONAL COVERAGES:
- Rental Car Reimbursement: Pays for a rental while your car is being repaired
- Roadside Assistance: 24/7 towing, jump-starts, tire changes, lockout service, fuel delivery
- Gap Insurance: Covers the difference between your car's value and what you owe
- Accident Forgiveness: Protects your rate after your first at-fault accident
- Better Car Replacement®: Get up to 20% more than your car's value if it's totaled within the first year

DISCOUNTS:
- Multi-policy (bundle with home)
- Good driver
- Good student (students with B average or better)
- SmartRide® (usage-based discount up to 40%)
- Defensive driving course
- Multi-car
- Paid-in-full
- Paperless/auto-pay

HOME INSURANCE

Protect your most valuable investment with comprehensive homeowners insurance.

STANDARD COVERAGE:
- Dwelling: The structure of your home
- Other Structures: Detached garage, shed, fence
- Personal Property: Your belongings
- Loss of Use: Additional living expenses if your home is uninhabitable
- Personal Liability: Protection if someone is injured on your property
- Medical Payments to Others: Guest medical expenses

OPTIONAL ENDORSEMENTS:
- Scheduled Personal Property: Extra coverage for jewelry, art, collectibles
- Water Backup Coverage: Protects against sewer and drain backups
- Home Systems Protection: Covers repair/replacement of appliances and systems
- Identity Theft Coverage: Helps recover from identity theft
- Earthquake Coverage: Protection from earthquake damage
- Equipment Breakdown: Covers mechanical and electrical failures

BRAND NEW BELONGINGS®:
Replace items with brand new ones (not depreciated value) for items less than 5 years old.

BETTER ROOF REPLACEMENT®:
Full roof replacement for losses over 50% (no depreciation for age/wear).
